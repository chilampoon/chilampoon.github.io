The first post after 2020!

Author: Chi Lam Poon Date: April 11, 2021  17:40:47

Finally, the first post after the pandemic began!!!

2020 was the most brutal year in my life because the then future was extraordinarily unforeseeable and unstable. Later when I was waiting for my goddamn visa😈, I heard that my ex-advisor got colon cancer and was suffering in the hospital. I was so shocked and sad for a few days. He is a very nice person and was the closest professor in my early college life, although we didn’t keep in touch afterward… Anyways, life and death are pre-ordained. What we can do is support.

And I found that if I am continually nervous or anxious, I am not able to focus. So, I played Zelda all the time ( ͡° ͜ʖ ͡°) Once the source of anxiety is gone, the brain still needs time (~1 or 2 months?) to recover, which means my mind still easily gets distracted. Some other people on the internet also said they could not work or study well when feeling anxious. I guess the brain’s reaction to this kind of negative emotion is releasing some weird substances, probably neurotransmitters or something, to keep the body ‘unbusy’. When the pressure source is removed, the body will gradually degrade these substances, so there’s a recovery period, interesting bs…

Hard to believe I’ve been through all the shits and am living ‘stably’ in 2021… But there was also something happy in 2020 - I participated in VCL and GSoC in the summer. It was such a relief that being busy and communicating with my teammates and mentors, and learning exciting stuff. Also, I just received my first dose of COVID vaccine yesterday (could only choose Pfizer :/). My arm got sore 3 hours later, other than that, I don’t feel any uncomfortable or tired. It’s either ineffective or lucky…

Hmm, time to do my homework. Miss the sea a lot, hope that I can go scuba diving again and finish the kite surfing course soon😢
